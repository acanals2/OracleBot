# ICP qualifying checklist (90 seconds)

Before adding a prospect to [pipeline-tracker.csv](pipeline-tracker.csv), score **5 signals**. **Send** if **4+** are yes. **Skip** if **fewer than 3**.

| Signal | What to check | Want |
|--------|---------------|------|
| Pre-launch or scale event | Site, X, changelog, testnet roadmap | Public launch in ~30–90d OR major scale push |
| Real product | Staging / testnet | Not a landing page only |
| Technical leadership | LinkedIn | CTO or eng lead who cares about reliability |
| Budget | Crunchbase / news | Recent round (Seed+ typical) |
| Right-side-of-the-line | Product description | They **operate** their platform; not engagement-farming / growth hacks |

## Disqualifiers (do not outreach)

- Stable post-launch, no upcoming scale event → no urgency  
- Pre-product two-person team → no budget  
- Enterprise incumbents → long procurement  
- Buyers whose product is follower/view/signup inflation for **other** people’s platforms  

## Tier hint

- **Pilot $10k** — need proof format, API-only personas acceptable.  
- **Standard $25k** — default for trading / signals **≤90d** from launch.  
- **Launch $50k** — press, regulatory, or multi-scenario rehearsal; reference [docs/02-concierge-offering.md](../docs/02-concierge-offering.md).
